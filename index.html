Phase intelligence

Your cycle shapes everything about your training.

Hormonal fluctuations across your cycle meaningfully change your strength, recovery capacity, pain tolerance, and energy availability. FlowState is the first training tool built from this truth up — not a generic plan with a cycle overlay bolted on top.

Menstrual Restore & rebuild. Lower-intensity, recovery-focused sessions. Days 1–5
Follicular Rising energy. Push volume and introduce new lifts. Days 6–13
Ovulatory Peak strength window. Heavy loads, PRs, high intensity. Days 14–16
Luteal Steady maintenance. Technique work, moderate load. Days 17–28
